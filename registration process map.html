Registration Process

This 4-step registration process ensures that you are properly registered, verified, and added to the WhatsApp group without any delay or inconvenience.

  • Step 1:

    Fill in the Registration Form

    To begin the registration process, fill in the required details on the registration page, including your full name, email address, phone number, and any other necessary information.

  • Step 2:

    Receive Payment Link

    Once you have submitted the registration form, a payment link will be sent to your registered WhatsApp number. This link will direct you to a secure payment gateway where you can complete the payment process.

  • Step 3:

    Payment Verification

    After you have made the payment, please allow 2-3 hours for payment verification. Our team will verify the payment and confirm your registration status via WhatsApp.

  • Step 4:

    Access to WhatsApp Group

    Once your payment has been successfully verified, you will be added to the WhatsApp group. You will receive a notification with instructions on how to access the group and connect with other members.
